Elevate Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Elevate is a workout, focus, wellness, and personal progress Android app. This policy explains what data the app uses and how users can contact us.

Data we collect or store

  • Account data: email, Firebase UID, sign-in provider, account status, and login time.
  • Profile data: name, age, height, weight, goal bodyweight, and preferences.
  • Workout data: exercises, sets, reps, weights, sessions, templates, history, goals, and analytics.
  • Focus and wellness data: discipline score, focus sessions, app limits, selected blocked apps, and related history.
  • Permission-based data: installed app names/package names, usage statistics, and Accessibility events only when enabled by the user.
  • Backup data: Google Drive backup status, schedule, time zone, last backup source, and backup file information.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not collect camera, microphone, contacts, SMS, call log, precise location, payment-card, or password data.
  • Accessibility is not used to read private messages, passwords, forms, financial data, or content inside other apps.
  • The current release has no ads, affiliate tracking, paid subscriptions, coaching, supplement sales, or equipment sales.

Why we use data

  • To create and manage accounts.
  • To save profile, workout, focus, wellness, and progress data.
  • To run workout planning, analytics, discipline scoring, focus sessions, and app-limit features.
  • To sync account and backup metadata using Firebase.
  • To create Google Drive backups after the user gives permission.
  • To protect the app and database from abuse.

Storage and sharing

Data may be stored on the user's device, in Firebase Authentication, in Firebase Realtime Database, and in the user's Google Drive if Drive backup is enabled.

We use Google/Firebase, Google Drive, Google Play, and Android services to operate the app. We do not sell data. We may disclose data if required by law.

Permissions

Usage Access, Accessibility, notifications, and Google Drive access are optional. If a user disables an optional permission, related features may stop working.

Retention and deletion

Local data stays on the device until the user deletes it, clears app data, or uninstalls the app. Account-linked Firebase data stays while the account is active unless deletion is requested.

Users can delete account-linked app data inside the app from Privacy & Terms > Delete Account & Data. Users can also request deletion by emailing theduskdynamicsproductions@gmail.com.

Google Drive backup files may need to be deleted separately by the user from Google Drive.

User rights

Users may request access, correction, update, deletion, consent withdrawal, or grievance review using the contact emails above. We may need enough information to verify the request.

Children

Elevate is not intended for users under 18.

Security

Elevate uses Firebase Auth, Firebase Realtime Database rules, Firebase App Check, Android security controls, and Google Play platform protections. No security method is perfect, so users should keep their Google account and device secure.

Changes

If Elevate changes what data it collects or how data is used, this policy will be updated before the change is released.
